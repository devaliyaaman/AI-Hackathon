📌 Problem Statement

Retailers often face significant challenges in accurately forecasting product demand, leading to issues such as overstocking or stockouts. These inefficiencies directly impact sales, increase operational costs, and reduce customer satisfaction.

Traditional forecasting methods rely heavily on historical sales data combined with manual adjustments. However, such approaches fail to effectively capture:

Sudden market trends
Seasonal fluctuations
External factors such as promotions and competitor activities

Store managers and inventory planners struggle to aggregate and interpret multiple data sources quickly, making it difficult to take timely and informed replenishment decisions. This problem becomes even more complex in multi-store retail chains, where inventory optimization must balance both localized demand and overall supply chain constraints.

As a result, poor demand forecasting leads to:

Lost revenue opportunities
Increased inventory holding costs
Reduced customer loyalty

Existing tools often lack:

Real-time insights
User-friendly interfaces
Explainable AI-driven recommendations

There is a strong need for a lightweight, intelligent assistant that leverages modern Generative AI capabilities to provide accurate, contextual, and explainable demand forecasts.

📊 Data Considerations

The solution will utilize the following data inputs:

Historical sales data (daily or weekly) for retail products
Format: CSV or JSON
Additional contextual variables:
Promotion schedules
Holiday calendars
Basic store attributes
Data Sources
Internal retail systems
Public retail datasets (if internal data is unavailable)
Data Characteristics
Moderate data volume suitable for short-term forecasting (3–6 months)
No personal customer data involved, minimizing privacy concerns
Data Preparation
Handling missing values
Identifying seasonality patterns
Basic preprocessing:
Date parsing
Normalization
Recommendation
Use synthetic or anonymized data for rapid prototyping
Simulate:
Demand fluctuations
Promotional impacts
🎯 Solution Expectations

The proposed solution should include:

💻 Interface
A web-based application or command-line tool where users can input:
Product ID
Date range
Contextual factors (e.g., promotion status)
🤖 AI Functionality
Generate short-term demand forecasts with numerical predictions
Provide concise, natural language explanations highlighting key influencing factors
📦 Core Deliverables
AI-powered forecasting engine using prompt engineering
Simple and intuitive user interface
Exportable forecast report
📈 Success Metrics

The effectiveness of the solution will be evaluated based on:

Forecast accuracy: ≥ 85%
User understanding of AI-generated explanations (via demo feedback)
Demonstrated reduction in overstock risk (target: 20% improvement)
🚀 Additional Notes
Integration can be limited to local data ingestion or a mock API
Emphasis should be on:
Simplicity
Explainability
Practical usability

Participants are encouraged to explore creative approaches that align with the problem context and enhance the overall effectiveness of the solution.
